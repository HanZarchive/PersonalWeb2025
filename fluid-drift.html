Fluid Drift

Individual Work · December 2023 - March 2024

Fluid Drift is a service designed for digital nomads, helping them build a sense of inner order and connection to reality to improve well-being.
The project features jewelry and fragrance kits, complemented by an automated vending machine service in various cities.
By engaging with adaptive, flowing scents, digital nomads can gradually adjust to their mobile lifestyle, fostering stability amidst constant change.

# What does the product consist of?

## Service

Every trip city has various self-service fragrance machines to help users customize a unique scent based on their impression of the city.

## Hardware

# How is it used?

## Necklace - Smell

The scent diffuses from the fragrance unit to the main body, then release into the air. It can mildly remind digital nomads of connecting reality.

## Bracelet - Relive

After leaving the traveling city, the corresponding fragrance unit is replaced, allowing users to string them into a bracelet, preserving the memories of their travels.
