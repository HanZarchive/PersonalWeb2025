# Technical Features

## LiDAR Sensors Array - Capture Dreams

The sleep mask is equipped with LiDAR sensors that can detect REM (Rapid Eye Movement) without physical contact with the eye. This technology monitors when a person is dreaming.

## DALL-E AI Model - Visualize Dreams

Creates and refines images from text descriptions. The AI model generates adjustable visual moon symbols based on dream characteristics:

  • Emotion Intensity
  • Luminous Intensity
  • Positive/Negative Emotions (Warm/Cool Colors)
  • Logic Intensity / Fullness Degree
  • Reality Relevance / Detail Richness

Prototypes

There are five main sections in Wonderland: recording dreams, browsing other's dreams, creation based on other's dreams, my own dream space, and notifications.
